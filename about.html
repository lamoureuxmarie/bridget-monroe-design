I graduated with a Diploma of Costume in 2011 from Ultimo TAFE, in Sydney

My Diploma focused on costume for performance.
The core techniques developed were in pattern making, draping, advanced construction techniques, millinery and hard and soft sculpture.
Alongside this we studied costume history, theory, design and management.

My work is mostly centered around drag, circus and burlesque but I'm always happy explore new styles and ideas.
